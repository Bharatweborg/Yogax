About Us

Discover our passion for yoga and wellness

Welcome to Haranathx Yoga

Founded in 2010, Haranathx Yoga is dedicated to providing a welcoming space for practitioners of all levels. Our experienced instructors guide you through each pose with mindfulness and care.

We believe that yoga is more than just physical exercise - it's a journey of self-discovery and inner transformation. Our studio offers a peaceful sanctuary away from the stresses of daily life.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced yogi, our diverse range of classes will help you deepen your practice and find balance in body and mind.
